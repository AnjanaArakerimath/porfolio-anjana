Dr. ANJANA R ARAKERIMATH

HoD & Associate Professor, MCA

I am Dr. Anjana Arakerimath, an academic professional with over 25 years of experience in the field of Computer Applications, serving as Head of the MCA Department since July 2007 and as an Associate Professor at Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering (PCCOE), Pune. I have played a key role in strategic planning, curriculum enhancement, and fostering a collaborative academic environment. Over the years, I have led several initiatives to strengthen academic quality, improve infrastructure, and contribute effectively to accreditation processes. I actively contribute to academia through invited talks, guest lectures, and academic reviews, and have been associated with Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU), North Maharashtra University, and other institutions in roles such as BOS member, syllabus expert, academic auditor, and committee member for affiliation, course closure, and faculty selection.

My commitment is to create a progressive academic environment that emphasizes quality education, practical learning, and the holistic development of students to meet evolving industry and societal needs."
